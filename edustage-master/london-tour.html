London Educational Tour

Explore London with cultural experiences, iconic landmarks, and enriching activities.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival

Flight from the designated airport. Upon arrival at London Airport, you will be received by our Tour Director, who will guide you through the tour and ensure a smooth start to your adventure.

Day 2: SOHO Tour

Start your day with a visit to the School & Cultural classes. In the afternoon, explore Trafalgar Square, Leicester Square, Chinatown, and Piccadilly Circus—famous for their vibrant culture and history.

Day 3: City Tour

Attend School & Cultural classes in the morning. In the afternoon, enjoy a guided city tour visiting Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, and a scenic Thames River cruise.

Day 4: London Eye

Morning cultural class followed by a ride on the London Eye. Enjoy panoramic views of the River Thames, St. Paul’s Cathedral, and the Shard.

Day 5: Science Museum

Morning cultural class focusing on innovation. Afternoon visits to the Science Museum, Natural History Museum, and St Paul’s Cathedral.

Day 6: Madame Tussauds

Morning cultural class with award ceremony. Afternoon visit to Madame Tussauds, Sherlock Holmes’ House, Regent Park, and the Queen’s Mary Rose Garden.

Day 7: Departure

After a week of learning and exploration, depart from London airport, carrying cherished memories and experiences.
